About TXWaterPro

The modern way to find licensed water and wastewater professionals across Texas.

What We Do

TXWaterPro is a public directory of licensed water professionals in Texas, sourced from TCEQ (Texas Commission on Environmental Quality) public records. We make it easy to search, filter, and contact licensed operators, irrigators, inspectors, and more — all in one place.

The water industry has historically been fragmented and hard to navigate. Hiring managers, municipalities, and residents deserve fast access to verified professional credentials. That's what we're building.

The Data

All licensing data is sourced from TCEQ public records. License numbers, types, and contact information reflect publicly available information. If you are a licensed professional and need to update your information, please contact TCEQ directly at tceq.texas.gov.

License Types Covered

We currently index professionals across 12+ TCEQ license types including Water Treatment Operators, Wastewater Treatment Operators, Landscape Irrigators, Backflow Prevention Assembly Testers, Customer Service Inspectors, On-Site Sewage Facility professionals, and more.

Built For The Industry

TXWaterPro is built by people with deep roots in the Texas water industry. We understand the pain of tracking down qualified, licensed professionals — and we're solving it with modern technology and clean, fast design.
